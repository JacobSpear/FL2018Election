District 30

In the 30th district, of voters who supported either a democrat or a republican, 52.85% supported a democrat and 47.15% supported a republican. There is a moderate correlation between party affiliation and voter turnout by precinct in this district. Specifically, there is a negative correlation between democratic party affiliation and voter turnout by precinct, with a correlation (pearson r) of -0.60. Moreover, a hypothesis test on the null hypothesis that the slope of the relationship is 0 gives a p-value of 7.1(10^-3).

Note: the p-value reported here is four times larger than the p-value reported by the jupyter notebook. This is because we are correcting for having run four hypothesis tests, one for each plot, to avoid a type 1 error.
